Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18 February 2026

At Lake Airthai, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our air quality monitoring and forecasting services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: To provide you with accurate, real-time air quality data (AQI, PM2.5) for your specific area, we may request access to your device's geolocation. We only use this information to identify the nearest monitoring station. We do not store your precise movement history on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Like most websites, we automatically collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our dashboard. This includes your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on those pages.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your preferred language, measurement units, or "favorite" monitoring stations.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To display localized air quality indexes and forecasts based on your current location.
  • Performance Improvement: To analyze user trends and optimize the dashboard interface and loading speeds.
  • Security: To protect our website from unauthorized access or cyber attacks.

3. Data Sources and Accuracy

The air quality and weather data displayed on this dashboard are retrieved from Thai Pollution Control Department, Thai Meteorological Department.

  • This data is provided for informational purposes only.
  • While we strive for accuracy, we cannot guarantee the real-time precision of every sensor or forecast model.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. However, we may use trusted third-party services, such as:

  • Analytics Providers: (e.g., Google Analytics) to help us understand how users interact with the site.
  • Map Services: (e.g., Leaflet) to display station locations.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Location Permissions: You can choose to deny location access through your browser settings. You will still be able to use the dashboard by manually searching for a location.
  • Cookie Control: Most web browsers allow you to manage or delete cookies through their settings.

6. Compliance with Local Laws (PDPA)

For users in Thailand, we process data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). We ensure that any personal data collected is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
